Payment
=======

At this time, several payment methods are handled in Sonata e-commerce. Whether you'd like to use credit card payment providers such as Scellius or Ogone, or simply Paypal, or even want the ability to handle check payments, you should find what you need in this section.

You may get more details about the architecture here: :doc:`../../architecture/payment`.
